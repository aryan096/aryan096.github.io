Where2CIT

Overview

I am currently working on a project to provide a live feed of all the computers in the Center for Information Technology (CIT) with the help of the technical staff (tstaff).

After completion, Where2CIT (placeholder name) will help students and faculty quickly find a vacant machine, visualize usage, and report problems easily with a form.
